AB

AB

Legal design is the application of human-centered design to the world of law, to make legal systems and services more human-centered, usable, and satisfying.

Legal design is a way of assessing and creating legal services, with a focus on how usable, useful, and engaging these services are. It is an approach with three main sets of resources process, mindsets, and mechanics for legal professionals to use.

Margaret Hagan Law By Design

As a Lawyer

My goal is to apply design principles to the creation of legal solutions. Applying legal design to legal documents such as contracts, I make them more accessible, easier to understand and visually more attractive.

Contract design is one of the most demanded types of legal design. Its also referred to as document design, since it can be applied to numerous types of documents, not just contracts. Contract design focusses on making legal documents more accessible and preventing misinterpretation from happening by offering both a visual and a textual explanation
